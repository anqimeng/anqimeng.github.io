Balenciaga 2020
Summer / Spring
Campaign & RTW

Demna Gvasalia
September/October 2019





Master Card in the form of an ID card, watch without time, phone in hand, brand name in computer language... Gvasalia successfully translated today’s social problems into fashion language in a futuristic sense.

What we design composes today’s society, and the society designs us in every sense. The models on the runway are who we will be in the future.
