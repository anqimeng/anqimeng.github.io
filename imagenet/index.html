ABOUT

→ According to Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs, when physiological needs and safety needs are fulfilled, people start to pay more attention to their spiritual desire, and this is when "cure" is not limited to medicines or folk remedies that helps to recover anymore. We seek cure for our daily pressure, for negative emotions, for our mental world. This is a small archive of the cures for different symptoms. There are six categories of the cures, respectively "Past", "Present", "Useful", "Useless", "Physical", "Psychological", from which the audience can tell the development of the society - people's life focuses have changed.

→ The entire interface design is simple and contemporary, mainly based on typography and the archived images. Audience can hover the mouse on the symptoms and desire to see the corresponding cure for each.
