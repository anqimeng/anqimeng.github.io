Consuming-19
Anqi Meng
The strange of this certain period, the strange of daily life, the strange of human beings..., all can be told from consumer behaviour.

The year 2020 has a quite different beginning due to the worldwide breakout of Covid-19. I collected the receipts from 23/01/2020 to 31/05/2020. During this period, I have experienced the virus breakout in China, shutdown of WUhAN, Australia’s travel ban on China, transiting through Thailand for 14 days to get in Melbourne, and Melbourne lockdown.

Social, economical and political changes of the certain period at the beginning of year 2020 can be noticed from this collection.
